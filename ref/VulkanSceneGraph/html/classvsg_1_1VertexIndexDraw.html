Detailed Description

VertexIndexDraw provides a lightweight way of binding vertex arrays, indices and then issuing a vkCmdDrawIndexed command. Higher performance equivalent to use of individual vsg::BindVertexBuffers, vsg::BVindIndexBuffer and vsg::DrawIndex commands.
