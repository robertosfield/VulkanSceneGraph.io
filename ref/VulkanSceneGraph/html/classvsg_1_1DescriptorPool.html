mutex used to ensure thead safe access of DesciptorPool resources. Locked automatically by allocatoeDecstiproSet(..), freeDescriptorSet(), getAvailability() and DescriptorSet:::Implementation to ensure thread safe operation. Normal VulkanSceneGraph usage will not require users to lock this mutex so threat as an internal implementation detail.
